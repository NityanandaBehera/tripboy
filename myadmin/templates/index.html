INTRODUCTION ABOUT US

“Where travel meets comfort.”

Hey there! Established in 2023, your travel buddy Trip Boy is here to provide you with a hassle-free & comfortable stay in Rishikesh! Rest your worries & enjoy your trip to the charming city of Rishikesh with peace of mind. We promise! You have Trip Boy’s assistance around the clock without exception!

Rishikesh is a city of many hues where you can experience adventure, culture, nature & peace; all centered around the sacred river Ganges (Ganga) & magnificent Himalayas because this small yet vibrant city is situated at the banks of the holy river Ganges & foothills of Garhwal Himalayan range. Known as the world capital of yoga and meditation, Rishikesh offers first-hand experience of cultural heritage and a plethora of activities for all.

Trip Boy is the best travel agency in Rishikesh that specialize in adventure & experiential activities in Rishikesh and cater's you customized travel solutions to meet your travel demands — anytime, anywhere (we mean it!) From exquisite beach camping to an exhilarating rafting experience in Rishikesh, we have it all. But it doesn’t stop there; our travel services include Rafting, Camping, Bungee Jumping/ Reverse Bungee Jumping, Giant Swing, Zipline, Flying Fox, Sky Cycling & much more.

Travelling can become tiring & often draining if you feel uncomfortable. That’s why we exclusively focus on comfort, hygiene, & safety so you can be carefree & immerse yourself in breathtaking activities in Rishikesh. Our certified guides & instructors ensure you see the unique side of Rishikesh at your pace; That’s why we provide you with an organized set of activities, so you don’t miss out on the chance to explore this pleasant city. Experience the magic of Rishikesh in its raw and untamed form with Trip Boy and find amazing things to do in Rishikesh. And, not something to brag about, but we do go out of our way to meet your travel demands and offer you top-notch services. To meet your travel needs and offer you a memorable experience in Rishikesh, we work in close knit with our service providers.
